A Pledge of Non-Disinterest

From: "James Lin" (zid@ms2.hinet.net)
To: zee-list

I pledge non-disinterest,
to the Star,
of the disarrayed factions of Discordia,
and to Chaos from which cometh randomness,
many T.A.Z.'s1 above all gods,
with childlike wonder,
unbridled enthusiasm,
and the pursuit of magickal illumination for all.

1 Temporary autonomous zones
