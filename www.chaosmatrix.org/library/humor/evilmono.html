EVIL MONOPOLY

A game for those aged from the cradle to beyond the grave. Players can choose to be either Satan (upturned crucifix), Baron Samedi (top hat), Cthulhu (tentacled mass), or Christopher Lee (pair of fangs)

The object of the game is to move around the board committing random atrocities until the other players are either dead or just cant stand your company anymore.

Players must bid for the purchase of city streets as they make their way across the board, when a player has acquired a complete set of three streets he can then build an extermination camp on one of them. If a player manages to build four extermination camps on a single street, he will then be allowed to open up an experimentation pen.

If another player lands on a street which you have already purchased, then you must subject them to an hour of torture. This should primarily be a physical torture such as the unforgiving administration of a 'Sunny Delight' enema, but it could also involve elements of emotional/psychological torture such as exposure to the films of Chris Tucker.

If another player lands on a street upon which you have built an extermination camp, then you must immediately gas them and round up their known family and friends.

If another player lands on a street upon which you have built an experimentation pen, then you must surgically modify your opponent until they are effectively dragged in to another species or sub species of humanity. Extra points may be awarded for detail, for example the introduction of exotic bacteriological agents to the central nervous system or the grafting of amphibian or reptilian aspects to the subjects torso such as gills, scales or tentacles would be most auspicious.

The square in the top right hand corner of the board is the Immanentize the Eschaton square, every time you pass this square you may collect 200 innocent souls.

Landing on a square marked 'Community Chest' will automatically result in the player prostituting his or her body out to the other players to do with as they will.
