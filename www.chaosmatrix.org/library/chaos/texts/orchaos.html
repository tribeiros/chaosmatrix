Oven-Ready Chaos Download Page

"Oven-Ready Chaos" is an updated, online version of Phil Hine's original chapbook version of "Chaos Condensed". It is not to be confused with the New Falcon Publications book Condensed Chaos; they are two separate things entirely. It has been changed to "Oven-Ready Chaos" on the internet to prevent further confusion.

It is available in Adobe PDF format for Acrobat Reader, which can be downloaded freely for any type of computer. Visit Adobe's Acrobat Reader Download Page to get yourself a copy of Acrobat Reader before downloading Oven-Ready Chaos.
